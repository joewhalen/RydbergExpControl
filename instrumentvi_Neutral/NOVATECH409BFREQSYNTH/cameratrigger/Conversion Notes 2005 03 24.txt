Sub-VI's or functions that CANNOT be used in RedMOTimagepulseblastR9 on Magnesium:
Updated 2005/03/24

- Anything to do with the Roper camera
- The SRS DS345 (arbitrary waveform) unless you bring over the SRS.
	(I have not yet tested it on this system)
- the NI 6025 card. it does not exist in our system.  I've disabled everything related to it that I could find, but in case I missed anything, disable it.
- DIO channel 4 and 7 do not work for the 6733, and DIO channel 4 does not work for the 
6713.
